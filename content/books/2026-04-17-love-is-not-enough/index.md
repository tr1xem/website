## Review

To be honest if you ask me whats the greatest book on relationship dynamics? I would blindly suggest this to you. Its compilation of case study of 5 peoples who are dealing with some kind of problems in their relationship and we hear their journey about improving them. 

There is a lot to take on from this book. It challenges the idea that love alone can sustain a relationship. There are things more important than love in a relationship like values, compatibility, and personal responsibility which are far more important in the long run. Its a practical, no-nonsense take on relationships that pushes you to think beyond just feelings.
