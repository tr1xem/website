Flux - Let the fire flow

2025-09-13tr1x_em 1 min read (132 words)

Flux is a hackable shell for Hyprland powered by ignis. It provides a simple and intuitive interface for managing and controlling your desktop environment.

Installation⌗ 

curl https://raw.githubusercontent.com/tr1xem/flux/refs/heads/main/install.sh | sh
