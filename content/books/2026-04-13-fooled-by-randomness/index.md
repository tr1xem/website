## Review

This book really changes your mental model and adds new perspective of thinking things like "Is the thing I'm doing is it luck or skill?" . There is far more to success or failure than we understand or think of it. Sometimes the people you look up to maybe just a side effect of being lucky.

This books talks a lot about the major outcome which means a decision might give a bad outcome or a loss right now but in the longer run you could be having much more profit. Our ability of handling loss is far weaker than you think you couldn't handle a loss for 4 months of 10% even if you know you would be getting 20% gain for rest of the 6 months. Its not really a finance advice but a life advice. A must read to be honest.
