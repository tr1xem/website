## Review

I dont read fictions much but this, it was a great book to read.

We follow the journey of a young shepherd , who was on a journey from Spain to Egyptian desert in search of a hidden treasure. The key take away from the book was the pursuit of of purpose is more important than anything in life. Overall a really inspirational book to be honest and a very positive one.

There is line from the book I really love which goes like

> Love never keeps a man from his destiny.
