## Review

A great book specially for teens on financial literacy, would say a must read.

Robert draws contrast between the  "salary mindset" and the "investor mindset" and lists out the thoughts which people overlook while trying to utilise there money in a better way . It distills financial literacy into simple, memorable principle most notably the distinction between assets and liabilities, and the idea that true wealth is built by acquiring income-generating assets rather than relying solely on wages. This makes it especially impactful for beginners who lack exposure to investing concepts.
